History

TechCon is a major technology conference that began in the early 2000s to connect tech enthusiasts and industry leaders. Initially small, it quickly grew to a global event by 2010, covering diverse topics like AI, cybersecurity, and IoT. During the 2020 pandemic, it adapted to virtual formats and now uses hybrid models. TechCon emphasizes sustainability and ethical technology, significantly influencing the tech industry and fostering a global community.

Mission

TechCon's mission is to connect and inspire the global tech community by showcasing the latest innovations, fostering collaboration, and addressing the ethical and social impacts of technology
